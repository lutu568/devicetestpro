Common Issues & Solutions

No Sound Signal

Check if your microphone is properly connected and ensure you have granted microphone access in your browser and system settings.

Low Volume

Adjust your system volume settings, ensure microphone gain is set appropriately, or try moving the microphone closer to the sound source.

Intermittent Sound

Check if your USB connection is stable, ensure no background programs are using too many system resources, or try updating your audio drivers.

Background Noise

Try using your microphone in a quiet environment or consider using a microphone with noise-cancellation features.
