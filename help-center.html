Microphone Troubleshooting

No Microphone Detected

  1. Check if your microphone is properly connected to your device
  2. Make sure you've allowed browser permission to access your microphone
  3. Try selecting a different microphone if you have multiple devices
  4. Restart your browser and try again
  5. Check if your microphone is disabled in your system settings

Microphone Not Recording Sound

  1. Check if the microphone is muted in your system settings
  2. Increase the input volume in your system sound settings
  3. Make sure no other application is using the microphone
  4. Try speaking louder or moving closer to the microphone
  5. Test with a different microphone if available

Poor Audio Quality

  1. Check for background noise in your environment
  2. Make sure the microphone is not covered or obstructed
  3. Try adjusting the distance between you and the microphone
  4. Check if the microphone needs cleaning
  5. Update your audio drivers

Echo or Feedback Issues

  1. Lower your speaker volume
  2. Use headphones instead of speakers during testing
  3. Move the microphone away from speakers
  4. Check if echo cancellation is enabled in your system
  5. Consider acoustic treatment for your room if problems persist

Speaker Troubleshooting

No Sound from Speakers

  1. Check if your speakers are powered on and connected properly
  2. Make sure your system volume is not muted or too low
  3. Verify the correct audio output device is selected in your system
  4. Try playing audio from a different application
  5. Restart your browser and computer

Distorted Sound

  1. Lower the volume to see if distortion stops
  2. Check cable connections for damage
  3. Try a different audio source to rule out the content
  4. Update your audio drivers
  5. Check if your speakers need service or replacement

Audio Plays in Only One Channel

  1. Check your system's balance settings
  2. Inspect all cable connections
  3. Try different content to ensure it's not the source material
  4. Test with different speakers or headphones
  5. Update your audio drivers

Audio Latency or Delay

  1. Close other applications that might be using audio
  2. Check for system updates
  3. Update your audio drivers
  4. Try a different browser
  5. For Bluetooth speakers, try moving them closer to your device
