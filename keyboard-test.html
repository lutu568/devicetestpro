Common Keyboard Issues & Solutions

Unresponsive Keys

Clean under the keys using compressed air. For mechanical keyboards, you may need to remove keycaps for thorough cleaning. For membrane keyboards, check for liquid damage.

Key Repeating

Adjust keyboard repeat rate in system settings. If the issue persists, it may be due to dust or debris under the key or a failing key mechanism.

Connection Issues

For wired keyboards, try different USB ports or cables. For wireless keyboards, check battery levels and try re-pairing with your device.

Driver Problems

Update or reinstall keyboard drivers from your computer manufacturer's website. For gaming or specialty keyboards, install software from the manufacturer.
