Common Issues & Solutions

No Sound

Check if your device is muted, verify connections, ensure the correct output device is selected in your system settings.

Low Volume

Check both system volume and the application volume. Some devices have separate hardware volume controls as well.

Distorted Sound

Lower the volume to check if distortion is volume-related. Update audio drivers or check for damage to speakers or connections.

Channel Imbalance

Check balance settings in your audio control panel. Verify that all connections are secure and properly plugged in.
